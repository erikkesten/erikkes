Brand & label

  • Graphic design
  • Print
  • Post-press

A visual identity redesign for a small-scale Swedish mustery, including logotype, label system for two flavors, bottle selection and a promotional brochure. The project is grounded in brand and market analysis, focusing on natural ingredients and local craftsmanship. Design, printing and finishing were made by me together with Emilia Larsson.
