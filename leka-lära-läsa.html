Book project

  • Graphic design
  • Typography
  • Print
  • Post-press

Design of a pedagogical book for Karolina Larsson with a focus on readability, structure, typography and color scheme. A consistent design language was developed to enhance the content and create an accessible reading experience. Design, printing and post-press finishing were made by me together with Maria Trevisan and Lea Henriksson.
