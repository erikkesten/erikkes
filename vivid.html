Vivid Logo Design


Tools

Affinity Designer

Adobe Photoshop

Description

This logo was created as part of a personal passion project for an esports concept team. The goal was to design a modern, monochrome logo and the primary challenge was to integrate the letters "VIVID" into the design in a way that felt cohesive yet abstract. The design emphasizes on minimalism and also allow it to adapt seamlessly across a variety of applications, from merchandise to digital platforms.
